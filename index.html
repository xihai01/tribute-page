Rex Lapis - Liyue's Founding Father

Rex Lapis is the God of Contracts who founded the region of Liyue. He also helped established the Golden House in Liyue which is the only location in Teyvat that mints Teyvat’s currency, the Mora. Rex Lapis was the Geo Archon and is the oldest (~ 6000 years) of "The Seven” in Teyvat. In addition to forming the geography of Liyue, Rex Lapis was a warrior who defended Liyue in troubled times such as during the Archon War, which was waged in the area that is now Guili Plains against other gods. In one battle, he successfully defeated an ancient god known as Osial, using massive stone lances to imprison the sea god, which is now known as the Guyun Stone Forest. In another, he sealed the Mountain Dragon Azhdaha under a tree in Nantianmen following the dragon's rebellion.
  • 3000 Years Before Archon War: Little historical records exists for Rex Lapis childhood; however, local legends states Rex Lapis was born into a wealthy family in Celestia where his father was one of the seven rulers of Teyvat.
  • 2984 Years Before Archon War: At the young age of 16, Rex Lapis left his family and travelled to the land of knowledge, Sumeru, to attend the Sumeru Institute of Academia. There he conducted research on various subjects such as science, mathematics, literature, amd alchemy.
  • 2970 Years Before Archon War: Rex Lapis finishes his studies and returns back to his family in Celestia. He finds out that much has changed and Celestia is in a desperate power struggle between rulers. His father is sick and before he passes away, he gives Rex Lapis his geo vision and gnosis.
  • 2770 Years Before Archon War: After 200 hundred years of widespread conflict and prolonged stalemates, a peace treaty was negotiated and signed. Widespread political reforms were made in the palace of Celestia with Rex Lapis ousting the 6 original rulers of Teyvat and replacing them with his own hand picked new rulers.
  • 1000 Years Before Archon War: One of the seven of Teyvat, the God of Wisdom, dies of under mysterious circumstances. This leaves an empty seat in Celestia and creates a power vacuum as nations become paranoid of each other. Trust erodes.
  • 500 Years Before Archon War: Rex Lapis narrowly avoids assasination while attending a peace summit in Celestia while small clusters of political unrest breakout in multiple regions throughout Teyvat.
  • Start of Archon War: After the Archon War broke out, Rex Lapis's close friend and ally, Guizhong, perished in a battle that laid waste to Guili Plains and Dihua Marsh. Afterwards, Rex Lapis moves their people south of Mt. Tianheng, to the place that is now Liyue Harbor.
  • 500 Years After Archon War: Rex Lapis forms a contract with the adepti to defend Liyue harbor as he continued waging war against his enemies.
  • 2000 Years After Archon War: This time period marked the end of the Archon War. Rex Lapis emerged as one of the seven victors. New rulers took the seats in Celestia and Rex Lapis aided with drafting a new constitution for Teyvat.
  • 6000 Years After Archon War: Rex Lapis is purportedly assassinated during the titular Rite, and his vessel — known as the Exuvia — was taken away to the Golden House by the Liyue Qixing. To this day, the suspect has never been found.
